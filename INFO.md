# Account Enable Disable Active Directory and EntraID.

This form enables or disables an AD account and the synced EntraID account. Als the current sign-in sessions will be reboked within EntraID.
For example, it is useful when an employee is terminated and their account must be immediately disabled and all the signedin sessions must be terminated.

## Business Benefits

This form can enable or disable an account without direct access to AD or EntraID. By controlling access to AD and EntraID, security is improved.
